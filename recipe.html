SHAWARMA RECIPE

This recipe is really easy and is the best for shawarma lovers out there

Ingredients

  • cabbage
  • shawarma bread
  • sauce
  • meat
  • carrot

Instructions

  1. Grate the carrot and dice the cabbage
  2. season and fry your meat
  3. peel the bread
  4. mix it all together with the sauce then arrange it in the bread
  5. put in oven for 15 mins
  6. enjoy!
